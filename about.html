About
The SMFA Media Stockroom is a School Museum of Fine Arts resource that rents out professioinal level artistic equipment to students. Located in the basement of the SMFA campus on the Fenway, the SMFA Media Stockroom is available for use to all students. The Stockroom is staffed by student workers and managed by faculty.
Hours
Monday - Thursday: 9AM-5PM
Friday - Saturday: 10AM-4PM
Sunday: CLOSED
Rental Policy
Stockroom rentals are made with your Tufts or SMFA Student ID. Present it to the student monitor and request all necessary tools and items. The standard rental period is a 2 day rental period. Unless an extension is requested, all materials must be returned by 5PM on the second day.
